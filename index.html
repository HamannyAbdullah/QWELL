QWELL Home Care

Nothing says comfort and convenience quite like home. Whether our parents want some at-home personal care, or are looking for assisted living solutions, we offer a comprehensive experience ranging from hairstyling and pedicures to home physiotherapy, nursing, and post-operative care. We’re available around the clock and all week to cater to their whims and needs.

QWELL Travel

Our parents are the original storytellers, and we are on a mission to create more stories for them to tell. QWELL Travel gives our loved ones everything from one-day trips to full on long vacations, giving them memorable experiences both locally and internationally. We fully organize and tailor getaways to provide healthy, happy, and worry-free travel.

QWELL Club

A space a to give back to yourself. We give our parents the environment to build new bridges in an active and socially focused community, with a wide array of activities varying from functional training to more artistic pursuits. This is the space to try new things.

QWELL Design Store

Bespoke home solutions to make life easier for our loved ones, based on their unique needs and comforts. From simple enhancements to retrofit furniture and all the way to research-based, custom-made home solutions; every parent’s individual lifestyle and health needs are covered. Launching soon...

We could all use a hand every now and again. QWELL Confidant gives our parents their own personal companion with a fully equipped vehicle. Whatever they want to do, wherever they want to go and whenever they desire; we will always be by their side to cater to their daily needs.
